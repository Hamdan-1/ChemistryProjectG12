About the Researcher

Hamdan Moohialdin, a dedicated student currently enrolled in Class 12BSB, approaches the world of chemistry with a fervent passion, particularly in its intersection with environmental science. Driven by an innate curiosity about the air we breathe and the ramifications of pollution on human health, Hamdan has embarked on a research project focused on the intricate chemistry underlying urban air pollution. This endeavor serves as a platform for Him to delve into the scientific intricacies of this pressing issue and share his insightful findings with a wider audience.
